a little bit about myself...

Hi! I’m a 17-year-old high school student who loves exploring art, science, and engineering. Most of the work you’ll see here focuses on my art, but a big part of my curiosity also comes from studying how things work, solving problems, and experimenting with ideas in science and engineering. Even when it’s not shown directly on this page, I spend a lot of time learning, building small projects, and diving deeper into topics like physics and math. For me, creativity and curiosity go hand in hand, and I hope this portfolio reflects the way I enjoy growing and expressing myself through all these interests.
